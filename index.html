Drawphone

A cute little multiplayer drawing/guessing game I made. Get a few friends with smartphones together and try it out. It runs right in your phone's web browser; no app download required.

Mutual Music

Using your Spotify accounts, Mutual Music finds songs that you and a friend both like, or your mutual songs, and puts them into a playlist.

Spyout

A recreation of the board game The Resistance I made in the style of my previous game, Drawphone. It also runs right in your phone's web browser with no app download required.

Spotify-in-Common

Select a few Spotify playlists, and get back a new playlist containing only the songs that are in all of the selected playlists. Good for finding songs that two or more people both like.

Connect 4

A project I worked on with my friend, Kevin Shannon. It is everything you could ever want from an online Connect 4 game. And the AI is terrifying. Now working in smartphone browsers too; click Play below to try it now!

Vidocracy

A web app I made to bring democracy to your party's YouTube videos. Go to a website on your phone, enter a code, and start voting videos to add to your party's queue.

Lansite

A web app I made for managing LAN parties. It helps connect party-goers together with messages, votes, and game finders. Lansite makes the party run smoother so that more time can be spent playing the games!

Red Team LAN

The group I founded to play computer games with my friends. We've had over 30 events since 2013. Check out the timelapses of the parties on the website; they're pretty neat!

Truman Registration Helper

Instead of typing in classes manually, I made a simple Windows program that types and submits them for you so you don't miss out on that last remaining seat. Try it out on the mock registration page.
